## ⚡ MISSION CONTROL (About Me)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Final-year Electrical Engineering student** specializing in Digital Electronics, FPGA architectures, and Autonomous Systems. Building deterministic hardware environments, high-frequency processing cores, and AI-driven control loops.

### 🌌 Deep-Dive Technical Profile
- ⚡ **Digital Systems & FPGA:** Specialized in hardware acceleration, pipelined architectures (e.g., CORDIC cores operating at **115+ MHz**), and Verilog-based RTL design.
- 💻 **Software & Programming:** Advanced proficiency in hardware-software co-design using **C/C++** for bare-metal systems, **Python** for modular simulations, and **MATLAB** for mathematical analysis.
- 🧠 **AI & Control Optimization:** Merging modern intelligence with classical systems, such as deploying **Neural Networks** to optimize dynamic **PID loops** for multi-terrain robotic applications.
- 🎛️ **Hardware & HMI Design:** Experienced in multi-layer, high-speed PCB layout within **Altium Designer** and **KiCad**, focusing on signal integrity, power distribution, and advanced graphical display interfaces.
- 📐 **Physical & Mechanical Engineering:** Utilizing **SolidWorks** to model, simulate, and design the physical chassis and mechanical structure of autonomous systems.
- 🏫 **Academic Leadership:** Contributing to the university community as a 4x **Teaching Assistant** at the *University of Isfahan*, tutoring 120+ students in Microcontrollers, Electronics, Circuit Theory, and Programming.
